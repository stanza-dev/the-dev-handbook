# Why Accessibility Matters

Accessibility (a11y) ensures everyone can use your application, regardless of ability.

## Who Benefits?

### People with Disabilities
- **Visual**: Blindness, low vision, color blindness
- **Auditory**: Deafness, hard of hearing
- **Motor**: Limited fine motor control, tremors
- **Cognitive**: Learning disabilities, attention disorders

### Everyone Benefits
- Users on mobile devices
- Users with slow internet
- Users in bright sunlight
- Users with temporary injuries
- Aging population

## The Numbers

- ~15% of world population has some disability
- ~8% of men have color blindness
- 100% of users will age

## Legal Requirements

- **ADA** (Americans with Disabilities Act)
- **Section 508** (US Federal agencies)
- **WCAG** (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines)
- **European Accessibility Act**

## WCAG Principles: POUR

### 1. Perceivable
Users must be able to perceive the content:
- Text alternatives for images
- Captions for videos
- Sufficient color contrast

### 2. Operable
Users must be able to operate the interface:
- Keyboard accessible
- Enough time to read
- No seizure-inducing content

### 3. Understandable
Content must be understandable:
- Readable text
- Predictable navigation
- Input assistance

### 4. Robust
Content must work with assistive technologies:
- Valid HTML
- Compatible with screen readers
- Works across devices

## Business Benefits

- Larger audience
- Better SEO (search engines are "blind")
- Improved user experience for all
- Legal compliance
- Corporate responsibility
